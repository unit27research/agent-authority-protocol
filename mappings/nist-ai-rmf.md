# Mapping: NIST AI RMF

Source: https://www.nist.gov/itl/ai-risk-management-framework

The NIST AI Risk Management Framework is organized around functions for managing AI risk. The Agent Authority Protocol can support AI RMF-style work by making agent authority easier to document before deployment.

## Govern

ARD supports governance by recording:

- agent owner
- human sponsor
- purpose
- delegated authority
- review cadence
- revocation path
- residual risk

## Map

ARD supports mapping by identifying:

- systems affected
- data classes accessed
- external effects
- delegation chains
- production, legal, compliance, financial, or customer-facing reach

## Measure

ARD supports measurement by defining:

- derived risk band
- evidence requirements
- approval gates
- logging requirements
- review cadence

## Manage

ARD supports management by defining:

- rollback plan
- kill switch owner
- incident owner
- authority expiration
- renewal limits
- escalation conditions
