# Example: LinkedIn Content Agent

## Classification

```text
A2-R1-D0
```

Derived risk: Low

## Purpose

Drafts LinkedIn posts for human review.

## Scope

- Can draft public-facing copy.
- Can use approved internal notes or prior posts as source material.
- Cannot publish.
- Cannot schedule posts.
- Cannot send direct messages.
- Cannot invoke other agents.

## Evidence

- Draft artifact
- Human review record
- Version history
- Source notes used for the draft

## Controls

- Human approval required before publication.
- Public claims require source review.
- Brand, legal, or compliance-sensitive statements require additional review.
